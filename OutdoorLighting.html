Jolin ground spike light with LED, solar-operated

Ground spike lights are always advantageous when you want to create a flexible lighting solution outside. They can be placed in any lawn or flowerbed and can be quickly fixed into the soft ground via the ground spike. When using solar lights you also avoid unnecessary cable tangling or an annoying search for a suitable power source as the light is charged by the power of the sun alone. There is also no need to consider the power costs and consumption. The solar light has an integrated twilight sensor and the light duration is 8 hours. If you remove the ground spike, you also have the option of fixing the light to a solid surface. Screw holes are located on the mounting bracket. With integrated Samsung LEDs!
