Vika aluminium LED ceiling lamp

Simple, modern and linear - this is the Vika LED ceiling lamp. The square, white painted aluminium body surrounds a satin white polycarbonate diffuser, which guarantees glare-free lighting. There are LEDs concealed behind the diffuser. They emit a very cosy, warm white light. Vika is suitable for installation on either the ceiling or the wall and is therefore extremely versatile. All the installation parts are hidden by the aluminium frame so that no screws are visible.

Arcchio Jelle LED wall light

Jelle convinces with a very aesthetic design and a particularly beautiful lighting effect that radiates pure cosiness. The wall luminaire is operated with LEDs, which are located behind the convex designed cover and illuminate the concave element on the wall. These LEDs are efficient and therefore both bright and energy-saving, which is exactly the combination that consumers want. Due to the panel-shaped design of the light body, the light can be emitted upwards and downwards without any glare. This type of lighting is very comfortable and can be used in the corridor as well as in living rooms and bedrooms. The harmonious expression is enhanced by the completely white surface of the aluminium - ideal for many modern rooms!
